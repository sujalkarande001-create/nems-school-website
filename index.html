About the school

A caring school for strong foundations.

New English Medium School focuses on the basics that matter most in early education: reading, writing, mathematics, spoken English, discipline, creativity and confidence.

Parents want a school where their child is noticed, guided and encouraged. Our website presents that same promise clearly, professionally and warmly.
